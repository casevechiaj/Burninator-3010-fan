# BOM

## Heat inserts
- 4x M3x4 on cowl
- 3x M3x4 on dock (stealthchanger)
- 1x M3x4 on dock (madmax)
- 2x M3x4 on stealthchanger backplate
- 2x M3x4 on MGN7H/MGN9H/MGN12H klicky carriage
- 4x M3x4 on MGN7H/MGN9H/MGN12H beacon/cartographer carriage
- 2x m3x4 on LGX Adapter
- 2x m3x4 on EBB36 mount
- 2x M5 on DraftShift Adapter

## Screws
- 2x M3x35 SHCS to secure cowl to backplate and MGN7H, MGN9H or MGN12H
- 2x M3x6 SHCS to secure cowl to backplate and MGN7H, MGN9H or MGN12H
- 2x M3x5 FHCS to secure belt clamps on MGN7H, MGN9H or MGN12H carriage
- 4x M3x10 to secure MGN9H or MGN12H carriage to linear rail
- 4x M3x6 to secure MGN7H carriage to linear rail
- 2x M3x6 SHCS to secure dock back
- 1x M3x5 BHCS to secure spring steel blocker
- 4x M5x8 BHCS to secure stealthchanger dock to DraftShift adapter and 2020 extrusion or crossbar
- 2x M5x8 BHCS to secure maxmax dock to 2020 extrusion or crossbar
- 2x M3x16 standoff on extruder back
- 2x M3x6 BHCS to secure EBB36 mount to standoffs
- 2x M3x6 BHCS to secure EBB36 PCB to mount
- 2x M3x6 BHCS to secure optotap to backplate
- 2x M3x6 SHCS to secure LGX adapter to cowl
- 2 M3x20 BHCS to secure beacon/cartographer to carriage (longer screws needed for CHC-Xl and Rapido UHF cowl)

## Others
- 1x 2510 fan 24V
- 2x 4010 fan 24V
- 3x RGB PCB LEDs (NeoPixel or simular)
- 1x PTFE tube 21mm (stealthchanger dock)
- 1x PTFE tube 25mm (madmax dock)
- 1x spring steel blocker 7x29mm cut from old tape measure (stealthchanger dock)
- 1x spring steel blocker 5x36mm cut from old tape measure (madmax dock)
- 2x magnets 3x6mm N52 (stealthchanger dock)
- 3x magnets 3x6mm N52 (madmax dock)
